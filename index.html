Haider Ali Mir is an award-winning music composer whose work spans drama, thriller, horror, cultural, and genre cinema. His scores are built to serve the story — to make music a narrative.

Winner of the Next Generation Film Award for Best Musical Score, his credits span a growing body of film and television work across international productions.
